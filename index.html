Directions

The Delta King hotel is located on the Old Sacramento riverfront at the end of K street. There is nearby street parking as well as parking garages. If you are staying in a nearby hotel, the walks are fairly short. The map includes routes to both parking garages.

Parking Garages

Old Sacramento Garage - Entrance on I Street between 3rd and 2nd

Tower Bridge Garage - Entrance on Capitol Blvd at Neasham Circle near the Tower Bridge.

Getting to Old Sacramento

From I-5 - Take the J Street offramp, go forward two blocks and turn left on 5th Street, turn left on I Street one block ahead and stay in the left lanes to reach the vehicle entrance to Old Sacramento at 2nd Street and I Street.
